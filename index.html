The Ultimate Pizzaguide

Where we cover all your pizza needs as a student in Trondheim.

Frozen Pizza

You can't go wrong with a classic frozen pizza! Enjoy a tasty Grandiosa or a juicy Big One. The perfect meal for a student, both easy, cheap, and delicious. Just remember that there is such a thing as too much of a good thing!

Homemade Pizza

So you're a crafty guy/girl that can make a pizza yourself? Swell! Just make (or buy!) a dough, and get crafty with the topping. I can recommend a nice pepperoni/beef mix, or the classic with rocket salad and parmesan. You can also experiment with different cheeses and spices. Find your favorite and enjoy it!

Italian Style Pizza

So your taste in pizza is a bit more on the expensive side? Not much can beat a good italian style pizza, with a crunchy crust and savory topping. Not everyone has a stone-oven in their home, but this can be circumvented with either a pizza stone or an appliance like the Pizza Dragon from OBH Nordica (The writer of this article highly recommends it!)

Restaurants in Trondheim

So you got money burning in your pocket? Why not let someone else make your pizza? There are multiple tiers of pizza in Trondheim, ranging from Una Pizzeria on Solsiden to the Pizzabakeren spread around the city. Everyone got a preference, and you are free to choose whichever you like.
